  • Two Components
    A signature which is also known as logo, comprises of two components – heroic and wordmark. Combining the two forms a complete signature as per Abbott guidelines.
  • Three Components
    A signature which is also known as logo, comprises of three components – heroic, “Life to the Fullest” Brandline and wordmark. Combining the three forms a complete signature as per Abbott guidelines.
  • Clear Space
    Refers to the minimum space between the border of the signature and margin of the document. Clear space is a squared area taking certain proportion of the height of the signature.
  • All Caps
    When the text is written in capital letters and is found in Headlines, Headline Highlights, Subheadings, and Business Descriptors.
  • Sentence Case
    When the text is a running text and not in capital letters and is found in Headlines, Headline Highlights or Body Copy when Mercury Text G1 is identified as the font family.
